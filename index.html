Who We Are

Since October 2024, Rocket City Racing has brought together motorsport enthusiasts in Huntsville for unforgettable race watch parties and events. What started as a handful of F1 fans gathering for the US Grand Prix has grown into a vibrant community of over 50 members who share a passion for racing.

We're a welcoming mix of casual fans and serious motorsport followers. Whether you're new to Formula 1 or can recite every race statistic since the 1950s, you'll find your place with us. Our watch parties blend great conversation with focused race viewing, creating the perfect atmosphere to enjoy every Grand Prix.

While Formula 1 is our main focus, our love for racing extends to IndyCar, endurance racing, Porsche Cup, and more. We're united by the thrill of motorsport in all its forms.

Das Stahl Bierhaus: Our Official Watch Party Venue

Since the start of the 2025 F1 season, Das Stahl Bierhaus has been our home away from home. This fantastic Huntsville venue doesn't just host our watch parties‚Äîthey go above and beyond by creating race-inspired food specials that match each Grand Prix location. From poutine for the Canadian Grand Prix to full English breakfast for the British Grand Prix and chicken and waffles for the Belgian Grand Prix, Das Stahl brings authentic flavors to match the racing action.

The team at Das Stahl has embraced our community, and their support has been instrumental in our growth. They provide an excellent atmosphere with great food, drinks, and the perfect setup for race viewing.

Ready to Join the Grid?

Joining Rocket City Racing is simple. We organize everything through our active WhatsApp community, where members discuss daily F1 news, coordinate event attendance, and share their passion for motorsport.

To join us, just fill out the form below and we'll send you an invitation to the WhatsApp community. There are no membership fees, no requirements‚Äîjust show up, enjoy the racing, and be part of our growing family.
